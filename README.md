*A socio-emotional wellness app designed to help you build confidence, connection, and healthier habits ‚Äî one small action at a time.*

GlowIn is a self-esteem and personal growth app built with **SwiftUI**.  
It helps users strengthen their socio-emotional health through **daily quests**, **mood tracking**, **journaling**, **volunteering opportunities**, and **achievement milestones**.

The design focuses on simplicity, encouragement, and small wins that build over time.

---

##  Key Features

###  Daily Quests for Self-Esteem  
- One small actionable task each day (ex: *compliment yourself*, *reach out to a friend*, *take a mindful walk*).  
- Encouraging micro-interactions to boost consistency.  
- Designed to build confidence through tiny, meaningful actions.

### Nearby Volunteering Opportunities  
- Browse volunteer events near you (location-based).  
- Encourages community engagement and meaningful contribution.  
- Perfect for users looking to build purpose and social connection.

### Mood Tracker  
- Quick daily mood check-ins.  
- Trend insights that help users understand emotional patterns.  
- Simple, friendly interface ‚Äî nothing overwhelming.

### Journaling  
- Reflect on your day or your quest.
- 
### Achievements  
- Earn badges for streaks, quests completed, mood consistency, and more.  
- Turns personal growth into a rewarding, gamified experience.
